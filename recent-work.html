Government Scheme

What is PM Surya Ghar Yojana?

Launched in February 2024 by the Government of India, this scheme provides direct subsidy to 1 crore households for rooftop solar installation with free electricity benefits.

💰

Direct Subsidy in Bank Account

Subsidy of up to ₹1,08,000 is transferred directly to your bank account after installation and inspection — no middlemen, fully transparent.

⚡

Up to 300+ Units Free Electricity

A 3 KW system generates ~300 units/month — enough to make your electricity bill nearly zero in most households.

📋

We Handle All Paperwork

From DISCOM application to net metering to subsidy claim — our team manages the entire documentation on your behalf at no extra cost.

🔌

Net Metering Benefit

Sell excess electricity back to the grid through net metering and earn credits on your monthly electricity bill, reducing costs further.

🏦

Bank Loan at Low Interest

Government-facilitated collateral-free loans at reduced interest rates are available for households who wish to finance their installation.

✅

25-Year Panel Warranty

All panels come with manufacturer warranty. We also provide 5-year full service & AMC support for your system.

🏛️

Official Government Source Visit pmsuryagarh.gov.in or call the national helpline 1800-180-3333. Akshat Udyam is a registered MNRE-empanelled solar installer under PVVNL and UP DISCOMs.

Subsidy Breakdown

How Much Subsidy Will You Get?

As per MNRE guidelines, the central subsidy plus UP state subsidy is calculated based on system capacity.

System Capacity Central Govt. Subsidy State Govt. Subsidy Total Subsidy
1 KW ₹30,000 ₹15,000 ₹45,000
2 KW ₹60,000 ₹30,000 ₹90,000
3 KW ₹78,000 ₹30,000 ₹1,08,000
4 KW ₹78,000 ₹30,000 ₹1,08,000
5 KW ₹78,000 ₹30,000 ₹1,08,000

* Subsidy amounts are as per current MNRE & UP State Government guidelines and subject to revision. State subsidy availability may vary. Final amounts confirmed after site survey and DISCOM approval.
