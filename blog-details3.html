Unlock Benefits with Government Schemes!

The central and state governments are committed to supporting citizens with various electricity schemes and promoting environmentally friendly solutions. Explore the links below for easy access to these beneficial programs and make a positive impact today!

Official Website Of Uttar Pradesh New and Renewable Energy Devlopmemt Agency

This platform provides comprehensive information on various government initiatives and schemes related to renewable energy, including solar, wind, and bioenergy programs. Access links to valuable resources, guidelines, and application processes to take advantage of these initiatives and contribute to a sustainable future.

Assistance Portal - Jan Samarth Portal

It is a comprehensive portal designed to connect citizens with various government schemes and services. This platform provides detailed information and application processes for numerous initiatives, including financial assistance, loans, and subsidies to support diverse sectors. Explore the site to discover how you can benefit from these opportunities and enhance your quality of life.

Solar Samadhan Portal

This is an initiative by the Uttar Pradesh New and Renewable Energy Development Agency (UPNEDA). This site offers resources and support for solar rooftop solutions, including complaint registration, status tracking, and feedback submission. It also provides access to guidelines, downloadable forms, and contact information to assist citizens in adopting solar energy solutions efficiently.
